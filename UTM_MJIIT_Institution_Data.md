## MJIIT Postgraduate Programs

### Total Programs Offered: 10+ Postgraduate Programs
**Duration**: Varies (1-2 years for taught programs, 3+ years for research)

#### Research Programs:

##### 1. Doctor of Philosophy (PhD)
**Code**: PMJGA3AKA
- **Duration**: 3-4 years
- **Specializations**: Multiple research areas aligned with MJIIT strengths
- **Requirements**: Proposal defense, original research, thesis submission
- **Supervision**: Joint Malaysian-Japanese supervision available

##### 2. Doctor of Philosophy (Engineering Education)
**Code**: PMJEA3AKA
- **Focus**: Advanced research in engineering education
- **Applications**: Curriculum development, teaching methodologies, educational assessment
- **Target**: Engineering educators, education researchers, policy makers

##### 3. Master of Philosophy (MPhil)
**Code**: MMJGA3AKA
- **Duration**: 2 years (4 semesters)
- **Entry Point**: Often leads to PhD progression
- **Format**: Research-focused with thesis

#### Taught Course Programs:

##### 4. Master in Sustainable Systems
- **Duration**: 1 year (2 semesters + short semester)
- **Total Credits**: 42
- **Focus**: Latest developments in sustainable systems
- **Multidisciplinary Approach**: Tools to address complex sustainability challenges
- **Accreditation**: Malaysian Qualifications Agency (MQA)

##### 5. Master of Sustainability and Environmental Sciences
**Joint Degree with University of Tsukuba (Japan)**
- **Duration**: 2 years (4 semesters)
- **Format**: 
  - Year 1: UTM, Malaysia
  - Year 2: University of Tsukuba, Japan
- **Degrees Awarded**: Dual degrees from both universities
- **Focus**: Solutions to global issues, especially in tropical Asia
- **Supervision**: Joint supervision by both universities
- **Research Components**: Sustainability and environmental research

##### 6. Master in Disaster Risk Management (MDRM)
- **Duration**: 1 year
- **Philosophy**: Balance between "Education/Training – Research – Practice"
- **Network**: DRM professionals from various countries and backgrounds
- **Partnerships**: Strong collaboration with DRM agencies, NGOs, local governments
- **Requirements**: Bachelor's degree with good honours, CPA 2.5+, or CPA 2.0-2.49 with 5 years work experience
- **Entry**: Recognized institutions accepted

##### 7. Master in Technology and Innovation Management
- **Duration**: 1.5 years
- **Target Audience**: Executives and managers seeking career advancement
- **Focus**: Business acumen and leadership skills
- **Specialization**: Technology-based leadership and management
- **Curriculum**: Based on leadership competency model
- **Fees**: 
  - Local: RM 18,670
  - International: RM 28,500

##### 8. Master of Science (Engineering Education)
- **Duration**: 1.5 years
- **Focus**: Advanced education in engineering
- **Career Paths**:
  - Lecturer/Researcher
  - STEM Educator
  - Engineering Education Consultant and Trainer
  - Policy Maker (education related)
  - Training Center Entrepreneur
- **Benefits**: Improve quality of engineering education, identify teaching-learning problems
- **Fees**:
  - Local: RM 18,670
  - International: RM 31,554
- **Requirements**:
  - Bachelor's in Engineering, Science, Computer Science, or Mathematics with good honours
  - Equivalent qualifications with relevant experience accepted
  - **International**: IELTS 6.0+ or TOEFL 550+

##### 9-10. Additional Master's Programs
- Various specialized master's programs in emerging technologies
- Interdisciplinary research focus
- Flexible delivery options
